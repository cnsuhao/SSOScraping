In CSA, breathing \nis interrupted by a lack of respiratory effort; in OSA, breathing is \ninterrupted by a physical block to airflow despite respiratory effort, \nand <a target=\"_blank\" style=\"font-family: Arial;\" href=\"http://www.apneaboard.com/wiki/index.php?title=Snoring\" title=\"Snoring\">snoring</a> is common.</span><br><br>\n\n\n<span style=\"font-size: 14px;\">\nRegardless of type, an individual with sleep apnea is rarely \naware of having difficulty breathing, even upon awakening.  Sleep apnea is\n recognized as a problem by others witnessing the individual during \nepisodes or is suspected because of its effects on the body.  <a target=\"_blank\" style=\"font-family: Arial;\" href=\"http://www.apneaboard.com/wiki/index.php?title=Symptom\" title=\"Symptom\">Symptoms</a>\n may be present for years (or even decades) without identification, \nduring which time the sufferer may become conditioned to the <a target=\"_blank\" style=\"font-family: Arial;\" href=\"http://www.apneaboard.com/wiki/index.php?title=Daytime_sleepiness\" title=\"Daytime sleepiness\" class=\"mw-redirect\">daytime sleepiness</a> and fatigue associated with significant levels of sleep disturbance.</span>\n\n\n\n\n\n<br>\n</div>\n<div class=\"clearbreak\"></div></div></div></div></div></div>\n                                        <div class=\"clearbreak\"></div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<div id=\"jsn-mainbody\">\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<div class=\"com-content \">\n\t<div class=\"article\">\n\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\n\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\n\t\t<div class=\"jsn-article-content\">\n\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<p style=\"margin: 0in 0in 0pt; text-align: center;\" align=\"center\"><span style=\"font-size: 10pt; color: black; font-family: Arial;\">Copyright Â© <a href=\"/../\" style=\"color: blue; text-decoration: underline;\">ApneaBoard.com</a></span></p>\n<p style=\"margin: 0in 0in 0pt; text-align: center;\" align=\"center\"><span style=\"font-size: 10pt; color: black; font-family: Arial;\"><br></span></p>\n<h2 style=\"text-align: center;\"><strong>Please help us...</strong></h2>\n\n<p>Apnea Board is an educational web site and we survive on the kindness of our members.
